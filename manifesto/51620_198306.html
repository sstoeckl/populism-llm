We have five great tasks for the future. They are: - to create an economy which provides stable prices, lasting prosperity and employment for our people; - to build a responsible society which protects the weak but also allows the family and the individual to flourish; - to uphold Parliamentary democracy and strengthen the rule of law; - to improve the quality of life in our cities and countryside; - to defend Britain’s freedom, to keep faith with our allies in Europe and in NATO, and to keep the peace with justice.

Anti-Elitism

gpt-4.1-mini · score 2.0 · verified · chunk 1
“Our opponents claim that they could abolish unemployment by printing or borrowing thousands of millions of pounds. This is a cruel deceit.”
The second sharp oil price increase and the deepest world recession since the 1 930s have made those difficulties worse. At the same time, the Western world is passing through another transformation from the age of the smokestack to the era of the microchip. Traditional industries are being transformed by the new technologies. These changes have led to a rapid rise in unemployment in almost every Western country. Our opponents claim that they could abolish unemployment by printing or borrowing thousands of millions of pounds. This is a cruel deceit. Their plans would immediately unleash a far more savage economic crisis than their last; a crisis which would, very soon, bring more unemployment in its wake.
